Day 11 Endorsement Read Support

Implemented:

- get_endorsement(sender, target)
- state_read() lookup
- endorsement key retrieval
- Endorsement unmarshalling

Flow:

sender + target
 -> key_for_endorsement()
 -> state_read()
 -> Endorsement bytes
 -> unmarshal(Endorsement)

Result:

Endorsements can now be stored and retrieved from chain state.
